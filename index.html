HOW TO PLAY

The Goal

Write the same answer as exactly one other player. Match everyone? Too obvious. Match nobody? Too weird. Hit the sweet spot and your word goes on the Shit List.

Act I — Build Up

A black card is drawn and a letter is rolled. Everyone secretly writes an answer starting with that letter.

Answers are revealed. If exactly two players wrote the same thing, that word goes on the Shit List and both score a point.

Act I ends when the Shit List is full.

Similar answers count. Extremely similar answers still count as a match — like Boobies/A boob, or Murder/Murdering. Use host controls to pair them.

Two pairs on one card. If two separate pairs match on different answers, both pairs win points and both words go on the Shit List.

Don't reuse words. Don't write a word that's already on the Shit List.

If there's more than one two-person match in the final round, only the first one goes on the Shit List — but all matches still score points.

Act II — Tear Down

A new card is drawn. Everyone picks a word from the Shit List. The goal is to pick the most popular answer.

The most common answer is crossed off and everyone who picked it scores a point. Ties mean nobody scores and the word stays.

Last word standing wins. That player becomes the Champion of that word forever.

Host Controls

After each round the host can override matches — tap two answers to pair them, or tap a paired answer to dissolve the pair.
